 <h2 id="similar-species">Similar species</h2>

 Lacking the bulbils of <a href="/taxa/Phymatoceros bulbiculosus">Phymatoceros bulbiculosus</a> and possessing small horns at maturity, P. oreganus could only be mistaken for immature <a href="/taxa/Paraphymatoceros pearsonii">Paraphymatoceros pearsonii</a>. The distinction is microscopic and based on the colour and ornamentation of the spores.
