<h2 id="distinguishing-features">Distinguishing Features</h2>

Forms small (to 3 mm) rosettes of bordered leaves, green to red toward the tips, blackish green to iridescent toward their base, on calcareous rock in shaded seeps.

<h2 id="similar-species">Similar species</h2>

Within BC, only Hennediella might be confused for its bordered leaves, but it is red in KOH, not reddish orange, and has plane leaf margins with marginal cells not larger than the medial as seen in section. 

<h2 id="habitat">Habitat</h2>

Shady, calcareous seeps
