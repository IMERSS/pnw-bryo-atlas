 <h2 id="distinguishing-features">Distinguishing Features</h2>

 The ascending yellow-green shoots of <a href="/taxa/Trilophozia">Trilophozia</a> quinquedentata are up to about 5 cm long and 2-to-4 mm wide. The leaves are broadly 3-lobed, asymmetrical and wider than long; the lobe tips themselves typically pointed (not round). Underleaves are absent and gemmae are infrequent and when present are angular, green or brown.
