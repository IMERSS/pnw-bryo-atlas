 <h2 id="distinguishing-features">Distinguishing Features</h2>

 Erect, rounded, orange-brown to yellow-green shoots with densely overlapping, concave, deeply bilobed (0.3-0.5x leaf length) leaves. Gemmae absent

 <h2 id="similar-species">Similar species</h2>

 Similar to <a href="/taxa/Sphenolobus minutus">S. minutus</a>, but overall coarser and larger (shoots 1.2-3 mm wide, vs. 0.4-1.5 mm wide in <a href="/taxa/Sphenolobus minutus">S. minutus</a>), gemmae always absent (occasional in <a href="/taxa/Sphenolobus minutus">S. minutus</a>)

 <h2 id="habitat">Habitat</h2>

 Acidic rocks, talus, boulder fields, cliffs, subalpine heath, and tundra; high elevations

 <h2 id="associated-species">Associated species</h2>

 <a href="/taxa/Sphenolobus minutus">Sphenolobus minutus</a>, <a href="/taxa/Tetralophozia setiformis">Tetralophozia setiformis</a>, <a href="/taxa/Dicranum">Dicranum</a> elongatum, <a href="/taxa/Macrodiplophyllum plicatum">Macrodiplophyllum plicatum</a>, <a href="/taxa/Polytrichum piliferum">Polytrichum piliferum</a>, Ptilidium ciliare, <a href="/taxa/Racomitrium lanuginosum">Racomitrium lanuginosum</a>
