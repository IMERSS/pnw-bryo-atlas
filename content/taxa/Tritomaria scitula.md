 <h2 id="distinguishing-features">Distinguishing Features</h2>

 A consistently three-lobed species of calcareous habitsts, T. scitula has leaves that are longer than wide on horizontal-to-ascending green-to-brown shoots up to 1.5 cm long. Underleaves are absent and gemmae are reddish-brown and angular, clustering on the leaf lobe apices. The lateral leaves are nearly symmetrical.
